**JetBrains UI is a distribution of React components** built with [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/), [Radix UI](https://www.radix-ui.com/) and [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/). Our goal is to provide you with high-quality, accessible UI components and 1700+ JetBrains IDE icons that integrate easily into your projects.

## Not a library—an open component distribution

Like [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/), **JetBrains UI is not a typical install‑from‑NPM library**. It's an open collection you can **copy, modify, and customize** directly in your codebase. This "open code" approach gives you maximum flexibility without wrapper overhead or styling workarounds.

## What's included

1. **Components**: Essential UI pieces with baseline styles inspired by JetBrains design system, built on [Radix UI](https://www.radix-ui.com/) primitives and designed to integrate easily into your interfaces.
2. **Icons**: 1700+ JetBrains IDE icons across 13 categories (general, database, plugins, nodes, etc.), each with light and dark mode variants and multiple size options.

## Why JetBrains UI?

1. **Open source & copy‑first**: Direct access to source so you can adapt components at their core to your design system.
2. **1700+ JetBrains icons**: Complete icon set from JetBrains IDEs with light/dark mode support, installable individually or by category via Shadcn CLI.
3. **Performance & accessibility**: Sensible defaults and patterns that keep your site fast by default.
4. **Built on Radix UI**: Accessible, unstyled primitives as the foundation for all components.
5. **Complementary to shadcn/ui**: Works alongside shadcn/ui, providing JetBrains-styled components.
